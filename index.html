C Laura Acion Resume

I am a tenured Associate Researcher at the National Research Council (Argentina) working at the Calculus Institute, University of Buenos Aires. I lead a health data science research group in formation. One of the main goals of the group is to switch from paid-walled science to open science.

Another of my main career goals it to improve data analysis teaching and practice. One of the roles I enjoy most is to help and mentor others. I am active in international collaborative teaching and programming communities with high diversity standards. These communities help me achieve my goals while giving back to the open source community, with a focus in Latin America.
